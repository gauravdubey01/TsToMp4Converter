# Privacy Policy for TS to MP4 Converter

**Last updated:** July 17, 2026

## Data Collection

TS to MP4 Converter does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information.

## File Processing

All video file conversion is performed entirely on your local machine. No files are ever uploaded, transmitted, or sent to external servers. ffmpeg is downloaded from [gyan.dev](https://gyan.dev) on first launch solely to enable local conversion.

## Third-Party Links

The app contains a link to [Ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/gauravdubey) for optional support. Clicking that link will take you to Ko-fi's website, which has its own privacy policy.

## Changes

If this privacy policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised.
